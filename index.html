1983

The 1983 Cricket World Cup is etched in cricketing history as one of the most memorable and transformative tournaments ever. Held in England, this edition witnessed a stunning outcome, as a spirited Indian team led by Kapil Dev defied all odds to clinch their first-ever World Cup title, creating a watershed moment for Indian cricket.

2002

The 2002 ICC Champions Trophy was a notable moment for Indian cricket as they were crowned joint winners with Sri Lanka. Led by Sourav Ganguly, India showcased strong performances throughout the tournament. In the semi-final, India defeated South Africa with a brilliant all-round display, particularly from Yuvraj Singh and Virender Sehwag.

2011

The 2011 Cricket World Cup was a historic triumph for India, as they won the tournament on home soil. Led by captain MS Dhoni, India defeated Sri Lanka in the final at Mumbai's Wankhede Stadium. This victory made India the first team to win a World Cup at home and marked their second title after 1983, cementing India's dominance in world cricket.

2024

India won the 2024 ICC T20 World Cup by defeating South Africa in a thrilling final in Barbados. India posted a total of 177 runs, with South Africa chasing 178 for victory. Despite being in a strong position at 151/5 and needing only 30 runs off the last 30 balls, South Africa faltered due to a strong comeback by Indian bowlers.
